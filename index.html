How SAM Works

SAM is a private community of fathers committed to intentional fatherhood. We believe the best investment you can make is in your relationship with your children. Members meet monthly in small groups of eight, facilitated by trained moderators who ensure every session is productive and confidential.

Beyond monthly meetings, members gain access to our private online community, exclusive events, parenting resources from leading experts, and a network of fathers who genuinely have each other's backs.

SAM is a private membership community for fathers who want to be more present, intentional, and effective in their role as dads. We organize fathers into "Core Groups" of eight members who meet monthly for structured discussions, support, and accountability.
